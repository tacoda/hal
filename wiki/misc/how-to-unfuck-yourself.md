# How to Unf*ck Yourself

A self-improvement mind-map (Matt Gray, Founder OS) of eight mindset shifts.

- **Remove negative people** — negativity is contagious; after meeting someone, ask "Do
  I feel energized or drained?" and cut those who drain you.
- **Be vulnerable** — vulnerability sparks meaningful connections; be real, open up,
  turn stress into collaborative problem-solving.
- **Grow your network** — everyone has valuable expertise; genuine interest expands your
  circle on autopilot.
- **Don't worry about what others think** — seek your own approval, not others'; nobody
  cares as much as you think (spotlight effect); self-respect is most rewarding.
- **Embrace solitude** — loneliness ≠ solitude; solitude is essential to knowing
  yourself; cultivate it deliberately.
- **Never feel sorry for yourself** — self-pity saps your sovereignty; use the suck as
  fuel to level up.
- **Life doesn't owe you anything** — nothing's free; go win it; expecting handouts sets
  you up for disappointment.
