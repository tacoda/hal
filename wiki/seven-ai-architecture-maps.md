# The 7 AI Architecture Maps Every Leader Should Know

A leadership framework — "from strategy to value: a governed, secure, scalable AI
architecture." Success formula: **control before autonomy; govern before you scale**
(more autonomy → more impact, more risk, more responsibility).

The seven maps, each with a key question:

1. **Strategy & Capability** — why are we investing in AI? (align capabilities to
   business outcomes)
2. **Reference Architecture** — how should AI solutions be built? (standard blueprint,
   reuse, reduce sprawl)
3. **Agentic AI Architecture** — what are AI agents allowed to do? (autonomy &
   authority boundaries, accountability)
4. **Governance Architecture** — who controls AI? (embed governance, ensure compliance &
   responsibility)
5. **Security Architecture** — how is AI protected? (threat vectors, data protection,
   secure data/models/AI supply chain)
6. **Data Flow Architecture** — where does the data go? (track from ingestion to output,
   privacy & sovereignty, lineage)
7. **Value Architecture** — what value are we creating? (link investments to measurable
   outcomes, ROI, business value & risk reduction)

**The approach:** align → assess → design → govern → operate → realise value.
"Strong architecture. Strong governance. Sustainable value."
