# World History

The human past on a global scale — how societies formed, connected, clashed, and changed
from prehistory to the present — plus the discipline of how we know it. This folder is the
college-level core, told globally rather than Eurocentrically. It draws on
[anthropology](../anthropology/index.md) and [biology](../biology/index.md) (human origins),
frames [religion](../religion/index.md) and [political science](../political-science/index.md),
and gives the AI era its place in the long arc.

## The shape of the field

History rests on [historiography and method](historiography-and-historical-method.md) — how
sources become knowledge. The sweep runs from [prehistory and human origins](prehistory-and-human-origins.md)
through the [agricultural revolution](the-agricultural-revolution.md) to the first
[early civilizations](early-civilizations.md), [classical antiquity](classical-antiquity.md),
and [the medieval world](the-medieval-world.md) — all bound together by
[trade networks and cross-cultural exchange](trade-networks-and-cross-cultural-exchange.md).
The modern world is born in [early-modern global connection](early-modern-and-global-connection.md),
the [Enlightenment and Industrial revolutions](revolutions-enlightenment-and-industrial.md),
and the age of [imperialism and nationalism](imperialism-and-nationalism.md), through the
[twentieth century's wars and Cold War](the-twentieth-century-wars-and-cold-war.md) to
[globalization and the contemporary world](globalization-and-the-contemporary-world.md).
[Big history and theories of history](big-history-and-theories-of-history.md) steps back to
ask what patterns, if any, it all follows.

## Canonical works

- [The Rise of the West](mcneill-rise-of-the-west.md) — William McNeill; a founding work of world history
- [Guns, Germs, and Steel](diamond-guns-germs-and-steel.md) — Jared Diamond; geography and unequal development (with its critique)
- [Sapiens](harari-sapiens.md) — Yuval Noah Harari; the popular grand narrative
- [The Age of Extremes](hobsbawm-age-of-extremes.md) — Eric Hobsbawm; the short twentieth century
- [Civilization and Capitalism](braudel-civilization-and-capitalism.md) — Fernand Braudel; the Annales longue durée
- [The Histories](herodotus-histories.md) — Herodotus; the founding work of history-writing
