# Personal Development

Soft skills, productivity, and self-actualization — the standard canon of working on
yourself: how you focus, build habits, find meaning, and deal with other people. Applied
craft/careers lives in [AI & the Organization](../ai-org/index.md) and
[Software Engineering](../software-engineering/index.md); this folder is the general
human-effectiveness layer beneath all of it.

## Productivity & effectiveness

- [The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People](seven-habits-of-highly-effective-people.md) — character over technique: the maturity continuum and seven principle-based habits, private to public victory to renewal
- [Getting Things Done](getting-things-done.md) — stress-free productivity via a trusted external system: capture, clarify, organize, reflect, engage — mind like water
- [Deep Work](deep-work.md) — distraction-free concentration as a rare, valuable skill; attention residue and the four rules for depth
- [Essentialism](essentialism.md) — the disciplined pursuit of less but better: explore, eliminate, execute, and the power of a decisive no
