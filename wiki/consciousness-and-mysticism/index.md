# Consciousness & Mysticism

Works on the hardest question there is: **what is consciousness, and what is the self?** The folder
spans the whole range — rigorous analytic philosophy of mind, empirical neuroscience, and the
mystical/psychedelic traditions that probe altered states from the inside. It borders
[philosophy of mind](../philosophy/philosophy-of-mind.md), [neuroscience](../neuroscience/index.md),
[psychology](../psychology/index.md), [Eastern philosophy](../eastern-philosophy/index.md) (the
no-self traditions), and [science](../science/index.md) (what counts as an explanation here).

> **A note on register.** These works range from tightly argued philosophy and mainstream
> neuroscience to speculative and frankly contested theories. The notes flag which is which — e.g.
> Jaynes's bicameral mind and McKenna's Timewave are **not** accepted; IIT is a serious but disputed
> theory. Read accordingly.

### Alan Watts — Eastern thought for the West

British-American philosopher (1915–1973) who popularized [Zen](../eastern-philosophy/zen-and-japanese-philosophy.md),
[Daoism](../eastern-philosophy/daoism.md), and [Vedanta](../eastern-philosophy/hindu-philosophy.md); his
recurring theme is that the separate "skin-encapsulated ego" is an illusion.

- [The Way of Zen](watts-the-way-of-zen.md) — Zen's history and spirit for the West
- [The Wisdom of Insecurity](watts-the-wisdom-of-insecurity.md) — security is impossible; live in the present
- [The Book: On the Taboo Against Knowing Who You Are](watts-the-book.md) — the illusion of the separate self
- [Tao: The Watercourse Way](watts-tao-the-watercourse-way.md) — Daoism, wu wei, going with the flow

### Terence McKenna — psychedelics & the archaic revival

Ethnobotanist and psychedelic philosopher (1946–2000); erudite ethnobotany braided with unfalsifiable
speculation (stoned ape, Timewave).

- [Food of the Gods](mckenna-food-of-the-gods.md) — psychoactive plants in human evolution & history
- [The Archaic Revival](mckenna-the-archaic-revival.md) — shamanism, psychedelics, cultural renewal
- [True Hallucinations](mckenna-true-hallucinations.md) — the Experiment at La Chorrera; a psychedelic memoir
- [The Invisible Landscape](mckenna-the-invisible-landscape.md) — novelty theory / Timewave Zero (not science)
