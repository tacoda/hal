# Science

Science as the **general discipline** — not any one natural science, but the shared method,
reasoning, and norms that make the whole enterprise work: how empirical claims are made, tested,
quantified, and self-corrected. This folder is the college-level core of *how science knows what
it knows*. It is the meta-field under the specific sciences — [physics](../physics/index.md),
[chemistry](../chemistry/index.md), [biology](../biology/index.md),
[neuroscience](../neuroscience/index.md) — and shares deep borders with
[philosophy](../philosophy/index.md) (philosophy of science), [statistics](../statistics/index.md)
(inference), [logic](../logic/index.md) (reasoning), and [engineering](../engineering/index.md)
(applied knowledge).

## The shape of the field

[Science](what-is-science.md) is empirical inquiry aimed at describing, explaining, and predicting
the natural world. Its engine is [the scientific method](the-scientific-method.md) — an iterative
loop of [empirical evidence](empiricism-and-scientific-evidence.md),
[observation and measurement](observation-and-measurement.md), and
[controlled experiment](experiments-and-controls.md), from which claims climb the ladder of
[hypothesis, theory, and law](hypothesis-theory-and-law.md). Getting from data to warranted claims
runs on [scientific reasoning](scientific-reasoning.md), on separating
[correlation from causation](correlation-and-causation.md), and on honest handling of
[uncertainty, error, and reproducibility](uncertainty-error-and-reproducibility.md). What *counts*
as science is set by [falsifiability and the demarcation problem](falsifiability-and-demarcation.md);
what science *produces* are [models and theories](models-and-theories-in-science.md). Science
changes through [paradigms and scientific revolutions](paradigms-and-scientific-revolutions.md),
self-corrects through [the scientific community and peer review](scientific-community-and-peer-review.md),
divides into [the branches of science](the-branches-of-science.md), and lives inside
[science and society](science-and-society.md).
