# Biology

The study of life — from molecules to ecosystems. Biology is where
[chemistry](../chemistry/organic-chemistry.md) becomes living systems; it underlies
[neuroscience](../neuroscience/index.md), and its central idea (evolution) and its
mechanisms (feedback, adaptation, networks) recur across
[systems thinking](../systems-thinking/index.md) and even
[AI](../ai/index.md) (neural nets, genetic algorithms). This folder is the college-level
core.

## The shape of the field

Life is organized by scale. [The cell](the-cell.md) is the basic unit; its behavior is
run by [molecular biology and the central dogma](molecular-biology-and-the-central-dogma.md)
(DNA → RNA → protein) and powered by
[biochemistry and metabolism](biochemistry-and-metabolism.md). Information passes on through
[genetics and heredity](genetics-and-heredity.md) and
[cell division and reproduction](cell-division-and-reproduction.md), and over deep time it
is shaped by [evolution by natural selection](evolution-by-natural-selection.md) — the
unifying idea — producing [the tree of life](the-tree-of-life-and-taxonomy.md) and the
[microbial](microbiology.md) majority. Organisms hold themselves together through
[physiology and homeostasis](physiology-and-homeostasis.md) (a biological control loop) and
defend themselves via [immunology](immunology.md); they live embedded in
[ecology](ecology.md); and we now read and write life through
[genomics and biotechnology](genomics-and-biotechnology.md).

## Canonical works

- [Campbell Biology](campbell-biology.md) — Campbell & Reece; the standard intro textbook
- [On the Origin of Species](darwin-origin-of-species.md) — Charles Darwin; evolution by natural selection
- [Molecular Biology of the Cell](alberts-molecular-biology-of-the-cell.md) — Alberts et al.; the definitive cell-biology reference
- [The Selfish Gene](dawkins-the-selfish-gene.md) — Richard Dawkins; the gene-centric view (and "meme")
- [Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry](lehninger-principles-of-biochemistry.md) — Nelson & Cox; the standard biochemistry text
- [The Double Helix](watson-double-helix.md) — James Watson; discovering DNA's structure
