# Engineering

Engineering as the **general discipline** — the method and mindset of building things that
work under real constraints — of which [software engineering](../software-engineering/index.md)
is one specialization. Where the software folders cover the craft, this folder covers the
timeless engineering ideas beneath it: designing under constraint, engineering whole
systems, and making them reliable and safe. It shares deep roots with
[systems thinking](../systems-thinking/index.md) (Perrow, Leveson) and modern practice like
[SRE](../devops-sre/site-reliability-engineering.md) and
[harness engineering](../harness-engineering/harness-engineering.md).

## The shape of the field

At the center is [the engineering method](the-engineering-method.md) — using science plus
heuristics to act well under uncertainty — whose defining move is
[design under constraints](design-under-constraints.md): satisfying
[requirements](requirements-and-specifications.md) within cost, physics, and safety limits,
trading off rather than perfecting. [Systems engineering](systems-engineering.md) scales
that to the whole across its lifecycle, held together by
[standards and interfaces](standards-and-interfaces.md). Because the world is uncertain and
things break, engineering leans on [modeling and abstraction](modeling-and-abstraction.md),
[margins, tolerances, and uncertainty](margins-tolerances-and-uncertainty.md),
[reliability engineering](reliability-engineering.md), [safety engineering](safety-engineering.md),
and [failure analysis](failure-analysis-and-root-cause.md) — all under the professional duty
of [engineering ethics](engineering-ethics.md).

## Concepts

- [The Engineering Method](the-engineering-method.md) — science + heuristics to cause the best change under uncertainty; design-centered, distinct from science
- [Design Under Constraints](design-under-constraints.md) — the art of the good-enough: requirements within a feasible region, trade-offs not perfection
- [Systems Engineering](systems-engineering.md) — engineering the whole across its lifecycle: the V-model, interfaces, emergent behavior (INCOSE)
- [Requirements and Specifications](requirements-and-specifications.md) — needs → falsifiable buildable specs; functional vs non-functional; verification vs validation
- [Reliability Engineering](reliability-engineering.md) — failure rates, the bathtub curve, MTBF/MTTR, redundancy, FMEA; the ancestor of SRE
- [Safety Engineering](safety-engineering.md) — hazard analysis, fail-safe, defense in depth, safety factors; why a reliable system can still be unsafe (STAMP)
- [Failure Analysis and Root Cause](failure-analysis-and-root-cause.md) — forensic engineering, five-whys/fishbone, the myth of the single root cause, blameless investigation
- [Standards and Interfaces](standards-and-interfaces.md) — how independently-built parts fit: standardization, interchangeable parts, modularity, decoupling
- [Modeling and Abstraction](modeling-and-abstraction.md) — reason before building: physical/mathematical/simulation models, first-principles, back-of-the-envelope, "all models are wrong"
- [Margins, Tolerances, and Uncertainty](margins-tolerances-and-uncertainty.md) — safety factors, tolerance stack-up, uncertainty propagation, error budgets, calibration
- [Engineering Ethics](engineering-ethics.md) — public safety as paramount; codes of ethics, whistleblowing, Challenger/Pinto/Therac-25, and the extension to AI
