# Neuroscience

How nervous systems compute — from ion channels to cognition. This folder is the
college-level core, and it is the field that *inspired* [artificial intelligence](../ai/index.md):
the [neuron](neuron.md) is the loose model behind the artificial unit,
[synaptic plasticity](synaptic-plasticity.md) is the biological ancestor of weight updates,
and the [visual system](sensory-systems.md) directly shaped
[convolutional networks](../ai/convolutional-neural-networks.md). The analogy is real in
places and breaks down in others — the notes flag which.

## The shape of the field

It builds up by scale. The [neuron](neuron.md) fires an [action potential](action-potential.md)
and signals across the [synapse](synapse-and-neurotransmission.md); synapses change through
[plasticity](synaptic-plasticity.md), the substrate of learning. Spikes carry information via
[neural coding](neural-coding.md). Neurons wire into [circuits](neural-circuits.md) and, at
larger scale, into the [organized brain](brain-organization.md) with its
[sensory systems](sensory-systems.md) and [memory systems](learning-and-memory.md).
[Computational neuroscience](computational-neuroscience.md) models all of this
mathematically — the two-way street with ANNs — and
[predictive coding](predictive-coding-and-cognition.md) casts the whole brain as a
prediction machine (a striking parallel to the next-token objective of
[large language models](../ai/large-language-models.md)).

## Concepts

- [The Neuron](neuron.md) — soma, dendrites, axon, resting potential; the brain's computational unit
- [The Action Potential](action-potential.md) — the all-or-none spike: Na⁺/K⁺ dynamics, threshold, propagation
- [The Synapse and Neurotransmission](synapse-and-neurotransmission.md) — transmitter release, EPSP/IPSP, summation; the adjustable "weight"
- [Synaptic Plasticity](synaptic-plasticity.md) — Hebb, LTP/LTD, STDP; the biological analogue of learning
- [Neural Coding](neural-coding.md) — rate vs temporal coding, population coding, receptive fields, tuning curves
- [Neural Circuits](neural-circuits.md) — E/I balance, feedforward vs recurrent, oscillations, attractor dynamics
- [Brain Organization](brain-organization.md) — cortex, lobes, subcortical structures; localization vs distributed function
- [Sensory Systems](sensory-systems.md) — perception as staged computation; the visual pathway that inspired CNNs
- [Learning and Memory](learning-and-memory.md) — memory systems, hippocampal consolidation, engrams
- [Computational Neuroscience](computational-neuroscience.md) — Hodgkin-Huxley, integrate-and-fire, firing-rate models; the bridge to ANNs
- [Predictive Coding and Cognition](predictive-coding-and-cognition.md) — the Bayesian brain, free energy, and the next-token parallel

## Canonical works

- [Neuroscience](purves-neuroscience.md) — Purves et al.; the standard comprehensive textbook
