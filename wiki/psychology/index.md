# Psychology

The science of mind and behavior. This folder is the college-level core. It sits between
[neuroscience](../neuroscience/index.md) (the biological substrate) and
[sociology](../sociology/index.md) (the social level), overlaps
[economics](../economics/behavioral-economics.md) on judgment and choice, informs
[philosophy of mind](../philosophy/philosophy-of-mind.md), and is the empirical grounding
under the [personal-development](../personal-development/index.md) self-help canon.

## The shape of the field

Psychology's [history and schools](history-and-schools-of-psychology.md) run from
structuralism through behaviorism to the cognitive revolution, all constrained by
[research methods](research-methods-in-psychology.md). The core topics: how we take in the
world ([sensation and perception](sensation-and-perception.md)), how we change
([learning and conditioning](learning-and-conditioning.md) — the parallel to
[reinforcement learning](../ai/reinforcement-learning.md)), how we think and remember
([cognition and memory](cognition-and-memory.md)) and where that goes wrong
([cognitive biases and heuristics](cognitive-biases-and-heuristics.md)), how we grow up
([developmental psychology](developmental-psychology.md)), what makes us individuals
([personality](personality.md)), how others move us ([social psychology](social-psychology.md)),
what drives us ([motivation and emotion](motivation-and-emotion.md)), what goes wrong and
how it's treated ([clinical and abnormal psychology](clinical-and-abnormal-psychology.md)),
and what makes life go well ([positive psychology and wellbeing](positive-psychology-and-wellbeing.md)).

## Concepts

- [History and Schools of Psychology](history-and-schools-of-psychology.md) — structuralism → behaviorism → the cognitive revolution; nature-nurture, mind-brain
- [Research Methods in Psychology](research-methods-in-psychology.md) — experiment vs correlation, validity, significance vs effect size, the replication crisis
- [Sensation and Perception](sensation-and-perception.md) — transduction, thresholds, bottom-up vs top-down, Gestalt, constancies & illusions
- [Learning and Conditioning](learning-and-conditioning.md) — classical, operant, observational learning; the RL parallel
- [Cognition and Memory](cognition-and-memory.md) — attention, memory systems, schemas, reconstructive forgetting, metacognition
- [Cognitive Biases and Heuristics](cognitive-biases-and-heuristics.md) — bounded rationality, System 1/2, availability/representativeness/anchoring, framing
- [Developmental Psychology](developmental-psychology.md) — Piaget's stages, Vygotsky's ZPD, attachment, Erikson, nature × nurture
- [Personality](personality.md) — the Big Five, psychodynamic & humanistic views, the person-situation debate, measurement
- [Social Psychology](social-psychology.md) — conformity, obedience, attribution, dissonance, Cialdini's principles, group dynamics
- [Motivation and Emotion](motivation-and-emotion.md) — drives, Maslow, self-determination theory; theories of emotion
- [Clinical and Abnormal Psychology](clinical-and-abnormal-psychology.md) — the DSM model, biopsychosocial causation, CBT and other therapies, diagnosis critiques
- [Positive Psychology and Wellbeing](positive-psychology-and-wellbeing.md) — PERMA, flow, character strengths, resilience, the hedonic treadmill
