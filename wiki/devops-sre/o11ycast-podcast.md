# O11ycast — The Observability Podcast (Heavybit)

**O11ycast** ("o11y" = observability) is a Heavybit-hosted podcast **exploring
the observability side of software development.** It is anchored by voices from
**Honeycomb** — hosts across episodes include **Charity Majors** (CTO), **Jessica
Kerr** (Engineering Manager, Developer Relations), **Martin Thwaites** (Developer
Advocate), **Ken Rimple** (Senior Developer Relations Advocate), and **Austin
Parker** (Director of Open Source) — in conversation with guests from across the
observability and reliability community.

The show is available on Apple Podcasts, SoundCloud, Overcast, and via RSS.

## What it covers

Episodes dig into instrumentation, telemetry, and the day-to-day practice of
understanding production systems — for example, a recent episode with Charity
Majors, *"Metrics Are Good, Actually."* The through-line is treating
observability as a first-class engineering discipline: how teams instrument,
query, and reason about live systems rather than guessing from dashboards.

This connects directly to HAL's notes on making systems (and agents) legible in
production — see [agent observability](../ai-platform/agent-observability.md) and the broader
[observability, evals, and experimentation](../ai-platform/arize-observability-evals-experimentation.md)
material.
