# AI Engineering Bookshelf

A photographed stack of AI/ML books grouped into five tiers (bottom = foundations, top =
strategy).

- **Fundamentals:** *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow*
  (Géron); *Data Engineering with Python* (Crickard).
- **Time Series / data:** *Storytelling with Data* (Knaflic); *Applied Time Series
  Analysis and Forecasting with Python*; *Modern Time Series Forecasting with Python*
  (Joseph); *Hands-On Simulation Modeling with Python* (Ciaburro).
- **System Design:** *Practical Machine Learning for Computer Vision*; *Designing
  Machine Learning Systems* (Huyen); *AI Engineering* (Huyen); *Solutions Architect's
  Handbook*; *Architecting AI Software Systems*.
- **GenAI:** *Generative Deep Learning* (Foster); *Generative AI with LangChain*
  (Auffarth & Kuligin); *LLM Design Patterns* (Ken Huang); *AI Agents in Practice*
  (Valentina Alto).
- **Product / Strategy:** *The Profitable AI Advantage* (Zwingmann); *Your AI Survival
  Guide* (Rashidi); *Reimagined: Building Products with Generative AI* (Shi/Cai/Rong).

A reading progression mirroring the learn-foundations-first idea.
