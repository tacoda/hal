# Business

How organizations create, deliver, and capture value — strategy, marketing, customers,
money, and the people who run it. This folder is the general business canon, distinct from
the AI-commercial notes in [AI Business & Strategy](../ai-business/index.md) (which apply
these ideas to AI products). It rests on [economics](../economics/index.md), leans on
[psychology](../psychology/index.md) (customers and persuasion) and
[UX](../ux-design/index.md) (customer empathy), and — per the emphasis here — foregrounds
**strategy, ethics, and customer empathy**.

## The shape of the field

[Business strategy](business-strategy.md) is the set of choices about where to play and how
to win — sustained by a [competitive advantage](competitive-advantage.md), sometimes reset
by [disruptive innovation](disruptive-innovation.md), and realized through a
[business model with sound unit economics](business-models-and-unit-economics.md). The
market side: [marketing and positioning](marketing-and-positioning.md),
[brand and growth marketing](brand-and-growth-marketing.md), and above all
[customer empathy and jobs-to-be-done](customer-empathy-and-jobs-to-be-done.md), turned into
the right thing by [product management](product-management.md). Running it well takes
[leadership and management](leadership-and-management.md),
[finance & accounting literacy](finance-and-accounting-basics.md), the
[lean-startup discipline](entrepreneurship-and-lean-startup.md) under uncertainty, and
[business ethics](business-ethics.md) throughout.
