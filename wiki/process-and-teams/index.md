# Process & Teams

How software actually gets built by people: the methods (agile, Scrum, Kanban, XP, lean),
the discipline of building **the right thing** and delivering work that matters to a
business and its customers, and what makes teams effective. This folder pairs the
methodology concepts with the canon that defined them. It connects to
[business](../business/index.md) (product, customers), [DevOps & SRE](../devops-sre/index.md)
(flow, delivery), [software engineering](../software-engineering/index.md) (the craft), and
[psychology](../psychology/index.md) (teams).

## The shape of the field

[Agile](agile-and-the-agile-manifesto.md) is the umbrella; under it sit the methods —
[Scrum](scrum.md), [Kanban & flow](kanban-and-flow.md),
[Extreme Programming](extreme-programming.md), and
[lean software development](lean-software-development.md). But method is not the point:
the goal is [outcomes over output](outcomes-over-output.md) —
[discovering and delivering](product-discovery-and-delivery.md) work that matters, sized by
honest [estimation and planning](estimation-and-planning.md), improved through
[retrospectives and continuous improvement](continuous-improvement-and-retrospectives.md).
None of it works without [team dynamics and psychological safety](team-dynamics-and-psychological-safety.md),
and scaling it invites real [critiques](agile-at-scale-and-critiques.md).
