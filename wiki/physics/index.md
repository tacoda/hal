# Physics

The study of matter, energy, space, time, and the fundamental laws that govern them. This
folder is the college-level core. It is the most mathematized science — resting on
[calculus](../math/calculus.md), [differential equations](../math/differential-equations.md),
and [linear algebra](../math/linear-algebra.md) — and it hands down foundations to
[chemistry](../chemistry/index.md) (quantum mechanics → bonding) and shares deep ideas with
[systems thinking](../systems-thinking/chaos-and-nonlinear-dynamics.md) (chaos) and
[information theory](../math/information-theory.md) (entropy).

## The shape of the field

[Classical mechanics](classical-mechanics.md) describes motion; its currency is
[energy and conservation](energy-and-conservation.md), and its deepest lesson is
[symmetry and conservation laws](symmetry-and-conservation-laws.md) (Noether).
[Thermodynamics](thermodynamics.md) and [statistical mechanics](statistical-mechanics-and-entropy.md)
handle heat, entropy, and the arrow of time. [Electromagnetism](electromagnetism.md) unified
electricity, magnetism, and light, studied through [waves and optics](waves-and-optics.md).
The two twentieth-century revolutions — [relativity](relativity.md) (the very fast and very
massive) and [quantum mechanics](quantum-mechanics.md) (the very small) — reshaped
everything, leading to [the four fundamental forces](the-four-fundamental-forces.md),
[particle physics and the Standard Model](particle-physics-and-the-standard-model.md), and
[cosmology](cosmology.md).

## Concepts

- [Classical Mechanics](classical-mechanics.md) — Newton, Lagrangian/Hamiltonian, phase space, determinism and its limits
- [Energy and Conservation](energy-and-conservation.md) — kinetic/potential energy, work, power, conservation of energy & momentum
- [Thermodynamics](thermodynamics.md) — the four laws, heat vs work, entropy, free energy, the Carnot limit, the arrow of time
- [Statistical Mechanics and Entropy](statistical-mechanics-and-entropy.md) — microstates → macrostates, S = k ln W, the partition function, entropy as information
- [Electromagnetism](electromagnetism.md) — E & B fields, Maxwell's equations, the unification of light
- [Waves and Optics](waves-and-optics.md) — superposition, interference, diffraction, the wave equation, resonance
- [Relativity](relativity.md) — special (constant c, spacetime, E=mc²) and general (gravity as curvature)
- [Quantum Mechanics](quantum-mechanics.md) — the wavefunction, superposition, uncertainty, entanglement, measurement
- [The Four Fundamental Forces](the-four-fundamental-forces.md) — gravity, EM, strong, weak; ranges, carriers, unification
- [Particle Physics and the Standard Model](particle-physics-and-the-standard-model.md) — quarks, leptons, bosons, the Higgs, and the gaps
- [Cosmology](cosmology.md) — the Big Bang, expansion, the CMB, dark matter & energy, ΛCDM
- [Symmetry and Conservation Laws](symmetry-and-conservation-laws.md) — Noether's theorem, gauge symmetry, symmetry breaking
